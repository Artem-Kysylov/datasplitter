Frequently Asked Questions

Is DataSplitter free to use?

Yes, it is a 100% free utility developed by Syntax Labs to help professionals handle heavy datasets instantly.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. DataSplitter operates entirely inside your browser sandbox using modern HTML5 Streams. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Will it corrupt special characters or encodings?

No. Our streaming engine via PapaParse preserves strict binary integrity, keeping your original UTF-8/UTF-16 encodings and quoted commas completely intact.
